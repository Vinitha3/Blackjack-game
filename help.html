HOW TO PLAY


Click "HIT" button to release a card.


Mind your score and keep it lesser than or equal to 25.


Once your score near to 25, click the "STAND" button, otherwise you may BUST when your Score greater than 25, if your score is above 25 it will bust and automatically stand button works.


when you hit the "STAND", the computer will play then the result comes based on scores.


A has 1,J has 11, Q has 12, K has 13 and other number cards have the equal score to their number!


To reset, press "Deal" and it works once after the results are published!
